How Canada's pension funds earn up to 29% returns every quarter - and how ordinary citizens can now invest in it

By Ari Rabinovitch Global News

Posted June 10, 2025 3:19 pm

Updated June 11, 2025 3:23 pm

2 min read

With current inflation at 3.2%, many people are losing their savings. But there is an exception - pension funds managed by Capital Systematics. Not only do they protect money from inflation, but they bring their investors up to 29% returns every quarter - on top of inflation.

These funds utilize proven strategies that were previously only available to institutional investors. For the first time, the Canadian government has approved a pilot program that allows 500 citizens to invest on the same terms as the country's largest pension funds.
